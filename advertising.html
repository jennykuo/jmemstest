Permissions


Photocopies for course or research use that are supplied to the end user at no cost may be made without explicit permission or fee. Photocopies that are provided to the end user for a fee may not be made without payment of permissions fees to Duke University Press.

Address requests for permission to republish copyrighted material to Duke University Press Permissions.
