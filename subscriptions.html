The Journal of Medieval and Early Modern Studies is part of the e-Duke Journals Scholarly Collection available online on HighWire Press. The collection includes online access to 35 Duke humanities and social science journals.

To access The Journal of Medieval and Early Modern Studies online, go to JMEMS at HighWire Press. Then click "View Online."
